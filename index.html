— Our Story —

A Gazebo Where
Heritage is Served.

Nestled at the heart of the city, Street Spice Co. was born from a singular vision — to take the soulful, chaotic joy of Indian street food and serve it within an atmosphere of quiet luxury.

Our gazebo is not merely a structure; it is an architectural homage to the jaali screens and haveli courtyards of traditional India, built from reclaimed teak and adorned with hand-carved motifs. Every beam tells a story.

Our spice masters source their blends from the finest spice farms in India, grinding each masala fresh each morning. From the first sip of masala chai to the last spoonful of biryani, you travel — taste by taste — through the royal kitchens of India.
