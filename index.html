— Our Story —

Nestled at the heart of the city, Street Spice Co. was born from a singular vision — to take the soulful, chaotic joy of Indian street food and serve it within an atmosphere of quiet luxury.

A striking midnight-black canopy with a sharp, matte silhouette. Warm Edison bulbs glow beneath the frame, blending industrial lines with a sophisticated, high-contrast urban energy. It’s compact, professional, and undeniably stylish.

Kerala’s heritage is a tropical legacy of spice trade and classical arts, centered on the coconut tree. Known as the "Tree of Life," the coconut provides the essential flavors, fibers, and timber that sustain the state’s iconic culture and backwater lifestyle.

Our spice masters source their blends from the finest spice farms in India, grinding each masala fresh each morning. From the first sip of fresh tender coconut to the last bite of our vibrant chaat, you travel — taste by taste — through the bold, urban soul of modern India.
